Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AET (Alter Ego Trust)?
An Alter Ego Trust (AET) is a legal vehicle for Canadians 65+ that allows you to move assets into a trust so they do not form part of your estate upon death. This completely bypasses the probate process and the 1.4% fee.
How much does estate planning cost?
We offer flat-fee pricing for all our standard packages (Wills, POAs, Rep Agreements) so there are no surprises. Trust planning is quoted on a project basis. Contact us for our detailed fee schedule.
Do I need to visit your office?
No. We are a fully digital practice. We can meet via Zoom, and we have protocols for remote signing and witnessing of documents in accordance with Law Society of BC regulations.
