Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Alter Ego Trust (AET)?
An Alter Ego Trust is a substitute for a will for Canadians aged 65+. It allows assets to pass to beneficiaries outside of your estate, completely bypassing the 1.4% B.C. probate fee, while maintaining privacy and control during your lifetime.
How much are legal fees?
